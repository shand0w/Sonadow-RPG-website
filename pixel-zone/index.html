About the game

The game is a simple 2D platformer slightly stylized on retro.

As in any platformer, your task is to progress through levels, kill enemy robots along the way, collect coins and not die along the way.
The game has no story line yet, but is being prepared to implement cutscenes.

In addition to (so far) not very interesting gameplay, the game has:
Simple level editor;
Possibility to apply your own skin in the pause menu and more.

Minimal Requirements:

// Windows/Linux/Mac:

GRAPHICS: Open GLES 2.1 or higher
DRIVE: 200 MB free space on drive
PROCESSOR: i3-Core or AMD Ryzen 7
SOUND: WASAPI/CONEXANT or other default audio driver



Android:

OS: Android 6.0
On systems larger than 6.0, you will need to manually allow access to storage and internet connection in the settings
GRAPHICS: Open GLES 2.0 or higher
DRIVE: 200 MB free space on drive
PROCESSOR: Qualcomn Snapdragon 710 (or an alternative with similar or more power)
SOUND: Default driver
